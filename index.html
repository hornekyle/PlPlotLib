PlPlotLib

PlPlotLib is a wrapper for PlPlot inspired by the interface of matplotlib. It is intended to fill the need for rapid feedback while developing numerical simulations, and does not replace more sophisticated packages such as matplotlib or even direct use of PlPlot.

While it is possible that the output from PlPlotLib may be considered as publication quality, no assertion is made along those lines. This software has been written in the hope that it will be useful, especially for students in numerical analysis courses based on Fortran.
